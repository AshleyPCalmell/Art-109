Best Party of YOUR Life

Walking into the party you were hesitant to begin with.

I mean the guy is not even really your friend, more like one of those acquaintances from class.

This guy invited you to one of his big frat parties, and since you had the weekend off it seemed like a good chance to socialize.

Walking in though you feel a bit anxious, are eyes all on you or are they just people scouting for a chance? Eyes linger while you walk through the door, trying to remain comfortable in this new area. In your discomfort, you need to make a decision.

Grab a Drink

You walk up to what seems to be the considered the bar, decked out with a giant Gatorade cooler with a paper slapped in front reading “Chad’s secret jungle juice”. Though the writing is a bit crude you grab a red solo cup and pour your drink. You feel an arm wrap around your shoulder, seems you’ve made a new “friend”.
